Why Urban Safety Matters

Urban areas concentrate opportunities but also face unique safety challenges that require comprehensive solutions.

With over 55% of the world's population living in cities—a number expected to reach 68% by 2050—urban safety has become one of the most critical issues of our time. Crime affects everyone, but disproportionately impacts vulnerable communities.

Effective crime prevention requires a multi-faceted approach that combines technology, community engagement, environmental design, and strategic policing.
